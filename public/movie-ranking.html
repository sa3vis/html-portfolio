The Best Movies According to David

My top 4 movies of all-time.

Star Wars

Best sci-fi film of all time. It didn't just create an interesting world, but an interesting universe

Indiana Jones and the Raiders of the Ark

The single best adventure movie. Interesting cinamatography and really just doesn't feel like there is a wasted scene. Makes the world seem greater than what it is

Seven Samurai

So influential and keeps you engaged. It really seems so different from what you expect an old movie to be

The Sting

Still the best heist/con movie
